BLE Beacon

About

Bluetooth beacons are hardware transmitters - a class of Bluetooth low energy (LE) devices that broadcast their identifier to nearby portable electronic devices. The technology enables smartphones, tablets and other devices to perform actions when in close proximity to a beacon.

How to use this app

On your smartphone, you must have BLE Beacon app installed.This application requires you to have bluetooth and the Internet turned on.When you are in the region of one or more devices, on your smarthphone will appear information about objects of that region.You can download app here.
